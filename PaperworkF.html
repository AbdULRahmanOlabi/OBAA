Documents Required For The Registration Of Students Transferred Form Other Schools

PaperWork:

  • The Student Receives a Notice of No Objection To Admission From Our School
  • Student Should Bring a Transfer Document + Academic Sequence Certified By The Directorate of Education From The Governorate To Which The Previous School Reporting To
  • Submit a Complete File That Includes The Following Documents:
    • 4 Personal Photos Size 3×4
    • Copy of The Father's ID
    • The Family Book + a Copy of The Father's Page + The Mother's Page + The Student's Page To Be Registered
    • Duly Certified Medical Report
    • Family Record Issued By The Respective Government Department
