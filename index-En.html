    Omar Bin Abd Al-Aziz Private School

    Educational Institution established in 2003 Located in Damascus Countryside. OBAA is a Grade 1-12 Carefully-Deigned Curriculum School That Focuses on Preparing Students To Be Innovative Thinkers and Visionaries

    We Provide World-Class Education Which is Unparalleled Among Private Schools in Our City. Our Student-Centered Curriculum is Designed To Reinforce Our Students With an Educational Benchmark Standard.

    OBAA’S Set of Core Values Governs The Staff as Well as The Management, To Implement Our Curriculum and Inspire Students To Accomplish Higher Learning Abilities So They Can Serve Their Community By Being Role-Model Citizens in Our Authentic Society

    Our Goals are in Full Alignment With The Syrian National Agenda For Education, Which Strive To Focus on Enduring Matters Such as Visioneering, Teams and Systems Building, as Well as Continuous Improvement in Learning Behavior and Growth of Every Individual Student.

    English Language

    Carefully-Selected Curriculum Has Been Adopted To Be Taught By Experienced and Highly Qualified Professional Educators Dedicated To Enrich Your Child’s Language, Critical Thinking and Communicating Skills. Those Mastered Skills Indeed Will Help Them Positively Contribute To The Community and The World.

    Public Activities

    To Implement The Goal of Building The Human Being, We Have Provided Several Playgrounds Such as Football, Basketball, and Volleyball, in Addition To Gymnastics and Aerobics Halls and Two Heated Indoor Swimming Pools For Use During The Cold Seasons

    French Language

    Kids are Being Introduces To French Starting From Early Ages in Grade 1 Through Grade 12, Using Friendly and Advanced Skill Based Curriculum. Highly Skilled Teachers are Helping Your Kids To Learn and Excel.

    The Library

    Our Students Periodically Visit The Exquisite Library. They Also Have Access To Variety of Literature, Encyclopedias, History and Scientific References and Books Whenever Needed

    Information Technology

    We Are Proud To Provide Advanced Computer Laboratories That Support Our Plan To Develop The Skills of Our Students, Learn New Technologies That Makes Students Up To Date With The World Changes and Prepare Then For University Since Their Childhood To Keep Pace With The Needs of The Century

    Laboratories

    The Five Highly-Equipped Laboratories are Dedicated To Provide The Safe and Friendly Environment For Teaching Chemistry, Biology and Physics

    Our Mission

    OBAA’s Mission is To Inspire Students To Achieve Their Highest Level of Intellectual and Personal Development Through Comprehensive and Technology-Enabled Educational Programs and Dedicated Teachers

    Our Vision

    OBAA’s Vision is To Be a Leader in Inspiring and Molding Tomorrow’s Innovative Global Leaders By Facilitating Their Holistic Development in The New World Knowledge Economy
