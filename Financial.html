The Student's Registration in This Service Will Not Be Confirmed Until The First Payment is Settled in Full By a Bank Transfer To The School Account Number 700175 at Al-Baraka Bank Showing The Students Code

The School Should Be Notified No Later Than if The Student Wishes To Withdraw From The Transportation Service No Later Than 31 July 2024 If The School Not Notified By 31 July 2024 The Amount Paid For The Transportation Service Will Not Be Refunded Because The School is Committed With The Transportation Contractor To Reserve a Seat For The Student Till The End of a Scholastic Year

School Offers 5% Discount For Siblings From The Total Amount of The Annual Fees Expect Transportation Fees

You Will Be Notified By an SMS Message on Your Number Retested With Us With The Due Dates of The Second and Final School and Transportation Fees Noting That The Final Installment of The School and Transportation Fees Should Be Settled One Month Before The End of The First Scholastic Semester as a Maximum

Refund Polices:

Based on The Ministerial Circular Number 2857/543(8/4) on 7/9/2021 Registered Students Fees With The Private, Educational Facilities Can Be Refunded According To The Following:

  • One Month Before The Beginning of The Scholastic Year 100% of The Fees Will Be Refunded
  • Less Than One Month of The Beginning of The Scholastic Year, 90% of The Fees Will Be Refunded
  • If The Students Did Not Show Up During The First Week of School, 50% of The Fee Will Be Refunded
  • If The Student Doesn't Show Up For More Than One Week From The Starting of Scholastic Year No Refund Will Be Made

Any Positive Recommendation/Suggestions From The Parents Contributes To Elevate The Educational Standards of The School are Welcome On The Other Hand Any Office To The School or its Teachers and Staff Will Result To Legal Liability
