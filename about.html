About Us

Vision and Mission of The School

OBAA’s Vision is To Be a Leader in Inspiring and Molding Tomorrow’s Innovative Global Leaders By Facilitating Their Holistic Development in The New World Knowledge Economy.

  • Your kids are Provided With Opportunities To Excel in Second and Third Languages, Mental Math, Sports, Computing and Social Intelligence Through Extra-Curricular Activities
  • Our Students are Inspired To Follow Ethical Principles and Accept Diverse Culture While Embracing Syrian Heritage and Cultural Values

Moreover, The Incorporation of These Enrichment Materials isn't Just About Fulfilling Academic Requirements But is Integral To Our Belief in Providing a Holistic Education That Prepares Students To Be Global Citizens, Capable of Engaging Meaningfully With The World Around Them.

OBAA’s Mission is To Inspire Students To Achieve Their Highest Level of Intellectual and Personal Development Through Comprehensive and Technology-Enabled Educational Programs and Dedicated Teachers.
